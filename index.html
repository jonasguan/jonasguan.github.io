Jonas Guan

Hi. It’s Jonas.

I work on AI systems, especially the places where they fail or become unsafe.

I’m a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Toronto, advised by Nicolas Papernot and William Cunningham. I expect to graduate in December 2026.

I’m currently a Student Researcher at Google DeepMind, developing epistemic reasoning and memory systems for LLM agents.
